# Client Onboarding Skill

## Onboarding Philosophy
The client has already overcome their skepticism to sign up. The onboarding's job is to
confirm that decision was right — immediately. Speed of first value is everything.
They should feel the system working within 24 hours of signing.

## Onboarding Sequence Structure
1. Welcome message — warm, specific, confirms what they're getting and why it matters.
2. Quick win email — one thing they can see working right away (agent live, form active, etc.)
3. What to expect email — sets timeline, removes uncertainty, reduces buyer's remorse.
4. Check-in at day 7 — personal touch, ask one question, invite a reply.

## Tone for Onboarding
- Warmer than sales copy. They're already a client — speak to them like one.
- Confident and reassuring. They took a risk. Validate it.
- Never overwhelming. One action per communication maximum.

## Language
- "Your system" not "our software"
- "Your agent" not "the chatbot"
- "You're set up to never miss another call" not "the feature has been activated"
